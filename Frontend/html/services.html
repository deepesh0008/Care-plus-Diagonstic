Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

Explore our wide range of diagnostic tests. We use advanced technology to provide accurate and reliable results to help you and your doctor make informed healthcare decisions.

Pathology & Blood Tests

Test Name Description & Purpose Price (Rs.) Action
Complete Blood Count (CBC)
No fasting required		 Measures red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. Used to evaluate overall health and detect a wide range of disorders, including anemia, infection, and leukemia. 400
Lipid Profile
10-12 hours fasting		 Measures cholesterol and triglycerides in your blood. Crucial for assessing your risk of developing cardiovascular diseases such as heart disease or stroke. 800
Liver Function Test (LFT)
No fasting required		 Measures the levels of proteins, liver enzymes, and bilirubin in your blood to diagnose and monitor liver disease or damage. 900
Kidney Function Test (KFT)
No fasting required		 Checks how well your kidneys are working by measuring levels of urea, creatinine, and certain dissolved salts (electrolytes). 850
Thyroid Profile (T3, T4, TSH)
No fasting required		 Measures how well your thyroid gland is working to detect hyperthyroidism or hypothyroidism, which can affect weight, energy levels, and mood. 600
Vitamin B12 & D3
No fasting required		 Measures essential vitamins critical for bone health, nerve function, and red blood cell production to detect deficiencies causing fatigue or bone pain. 1200

Imaging & Radiology

Scan Name Description & Purpose Price (Rs.) Action
Digital X-Ray
Doctor prescription required		 Uses electromagnetic waves to create images of the inside of your body, most commonly used to check for fractures or lung infections. 500
MRI Scan
Doctor prescription required		 Uses strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce highly detailed images of internal organs, brain, and soft tissues. 4500
CT Scan
Doctor prescription required		 Combines multiple X-ray images taken from different angles to create cross-sectional (3D) images of bones, blood vessels, and soft tissues. 3500
Ultrasound (Sonography)
Doctor prescription required		 Uses high-frequency sound waves to capture live images from the inside of your body. Safe and painless, often used during pregnancy or for abdominal checks. 1200

Diabetes & Endocrinology

Test Name Description & Purpose Price (Rs.) Action
Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS)
8-10 hours fasting		 Measures your blood sugar after an overnight fast. A primary test used to diagnose diabetes and prediabetes. 120
Post Prandial Sugar (PP)
Exactly 2 hrs after meal		 Measures blood sugar exactly two hours after you start eating a meal to see how your body responds to sugar and starch. 150
HbA1c (Glycosylated Hemoglobin)
No fasting required		 Provides an average of your blood sugar control over the past 2 to 3 months. Essential for long-term diabetes management. 500
